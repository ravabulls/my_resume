M.E. thesis

Optimization Based Controller Design For Dead Time Systems

  • In this study, a simple design method of proportional-integral-derivative (PID) controller proposed for higher-order (HO)-plus delay time (HOPDT) processes.
  • This controller is designed to manage higher-order processes with long-dead times, long time constants, and monotonic or oscillatory responses.
  • The method is as per the real and imaginary values of the higher-order processes for the desired settling time and constraints on the complementary sensitivity function to handle the high-frequency noise rejection.
  • The procedure seems to be more straightforward, practical and enhanced performance can be expected of the various processes.
